Set up instructions.

*Our web site is compatible with Tasmota based sockets.

  • Add smart socket on our web site.
  • Proceed with initial configuration of your smart socket. Take a note of what your socket IP address is.
  • In Tasmota socket web interface enter configuration/MQTT and populate Topic field with the name of your smart socket as named in your entry on our web site. Make sure to populate Full Topic field with the following username/%topic%/%prefix%/ , where username is your username as registered on our site.
  • Enjoy.

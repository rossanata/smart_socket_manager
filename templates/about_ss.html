About smart sockets.

A smart socket can turn any of your home appliances into smart products, letting you control them even when you are away from home or in another room. You can also plan ahead and schedule the sockets to turn on and off at certain times. These small devices are easy to use and incredibly versatile since they can be used with almost any plug-in device. {# They are quite affordable too. Smart sockets stand out due to their ability to give#} {# users#} {# more control over ‘dumb’ devices, effectively bridging the gap between old and new, making them part#} {# of your#} {# smart home.#}

Here are some ideas about using smart sockets:

  • Prepare a meal ahead of time (in case you have a slow cooker)
  • Limit children screen time (with a timer or schedule)
  • Set the perfect temperature (schedule your AC or turn it on while away)
  • Control your night lights
  • Automate your Christmas/decoration lights
  • Make it look like you are home when you are away (by creating a schedule for visible from outside lights while you are away)
